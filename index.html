Free bass tabs that actually focus on the bass.

I made this site because a lot of “free tab” resources are really full-band transcriptions that gloss over what the bass is doing — the little movements, the feel, and the parts that make the groove work. Everything here is written specifically for bass and shared for free.

What you’ll find here

  • Clean, readable PDFs Great on phone/tablet, easy to print.
  • Guitar Pro files where available Slow it down, loop sections, change tempo.
  • Notes that help you play it right Tuning, technique hints, and “watch out for this bar” pointers.
